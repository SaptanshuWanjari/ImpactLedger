                                    CHAPTER 8
                             RESULTS AND IMPACT


The ImpactLedger platform demonstrates satisfactory performance across its core
functional areas. API response times for standard data retrieval operations were
observed to be within acceptable limits during testing, and the application
maintained stability under concurrent access by multiple test users. The frontend
renders correctly across desktop and mobile browsers, confirming the
responsiveness of the interface. Data submitted through forms is accurately stored
and retrieved, and the dashboard visualizations update in real time as new records
are added.


Feedback gathered from volunteers and administrators associated with Lions Club
Nagpur Sawaj indicated a positive reception of the platform. Users reported that the
project dashboard made it easier to understand the status of ongoing initiatives at a
glance, and that the activity logging feature simplified the process of maintaining
records. Donor-facing views were appreciated for providing a clear summary of
contributions and their associated outcomes, increasing confidence in the platform's
transparency.


Prior to the implementation of ImpactLedger, the partner organization managed
project records using physical registers and informal WhatsApp communications.
This approach led to delays in consolidating data, inconsistencies in reports, and
difficulty in demonstrating impact to external stakeholders. Following the
introduction of the platform, these processes are conducted digitally, reducing the
time required for report generation and improving the accuracy of data across all
project activities.
