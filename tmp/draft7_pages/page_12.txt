The core problem addressed by this project is the lack of structured transparency
mechanisms in community initiatives, which results in mismanagement of
resources, weakened donor relationships, and inefficient impact reporting.
ImpactLedger aims to resolve this by providing a reliable digital ecosystem for
NGO operations.


The scope of the project covers the complete design and development of a web
application with features for user authentication, project creation, activity tracking,
donation recording, and data visualization. The system is intended for use by NGOs
registered on the platform and their associated stakeholders.
